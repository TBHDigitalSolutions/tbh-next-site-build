## Overview

Your teams need **consistent, on-brand materials** that are fast to update and easy to ship. The **Brand Collateral Kit** delivers the core set as **editable templates**—with press-ready and web-ready outputs—so Sales, Marketing, and Execs can move quickly without breaking the brand.

### What you’ll get

- A prioritized collateral set aligned to real use-cases  
- **Press-ready** files for print and **optimized** assets for web  
- Editable sources in **Figma/Adobe** and a **brand guide lite**  
- A short live handoff so your team knows how to use and update everything

---

## Purpose

Establish a **reusable template system** and **single source of truth** for everyday collateral—reducing one-off design requests, improving quality, and speeding time-to-send for campaigns, outreach, and events.

---

## FAQ

**Which formats do we receive?**  
Editable sources (Figma/Adobe), press-ready PDFs with bleed, and optimized PNG/SVG/WEBP for digital.

**Can you adapt the slide master to PowerPoint or Google Slides?**  
Yes. We’ll adapt the master and validate key layouts. Minor fidelity differences may apply.

**Do you include fonts?**  
We use your licensed brand fonts; if licensing is needed we’ll recommend options (fees paid by you).

**Can we localize templates?**  
Yes—localization and regional adaptations can be scoped per market.

---

## Notes

Two structured review rounds are included. Stock/illustration licensing and custom iconography beyond 12 glyphs are add-ons. If you need **event signage**, **tradeshow assets**, or **advanced print finishes**, we can extend the kit or schedule a follow-on mini-sprint.
