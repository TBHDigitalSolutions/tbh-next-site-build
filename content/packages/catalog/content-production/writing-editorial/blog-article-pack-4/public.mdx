## Overview

A reliable monthly **blog engine**: we plan topics, draft SEO-aligned articles, and ship polished, on-brand content ready to publish. You get **consistent cadence**, clean structure, and measurable outcomes — without adding headcount.

### Who it’s for

Teams that need predictable, high-quality publishing (weekly) to build topical authority and capture search demand.

---

## Purpose

Create a **predictable pipeline** from brief → draft → optimized article so you publish high-signal content every week with clear on-page SEO, stronger internal linking, and smooth CMS handoff. The goal: compounding organic growth and a team that ships on schedule.

---

## Why this matters

- Search visibility compounds with **consistent, intent-matched content**.  
- Clear, SEO-grounded briefs keep output **on strategy** and reduce rewrites.  
- A weekly cadence builds **audience habit and internal momentum**.

> Result: organic growth you can forecast and scale.

- **4 articles/month** (≈800–1,200 words each)

## FAQ

**Can you write longer or shorter pieces?**  
Yes. Length can vary per piece; long-form may affect capacity or require scope changes.

**Do you publish in our CMS?**  
Optional. We can deliver CMS-ready assets or publish with access and a staging workflow.

**Can topics change mid-month?**  
Yes, within the planning window. Major changes after drafting may consume a revision round.

**Do you handle image sourcing?**  
We provide suggestions and alt text guidance; licensing/stock purchase is the client’s responsibility.
