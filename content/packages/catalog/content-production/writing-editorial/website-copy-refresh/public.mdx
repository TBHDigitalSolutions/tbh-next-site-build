## Overview

**Website Copy Refresh** focuses effort where it matters most—your high-impact pages. We align voice and structure, improve scan-ability and hierarchy, clean up on-page SEO, and refine CTAs and microcopy. The result: clearer value communication and fewer review loops, without a full redesign.

### What you’ll get

- Tracked-changes edits or **CMS comments** for up to **5 pages**  
- Updated **titles, metas, H1–H3**, and internal linking suggestions  
- **CTA/microcopy** tuned for conversion and reduced friction  
- An **editor’s checklist** and **implementation notes** to keep it consistent

## Purpose

Make your top pages **clear, consistent, and conversion-ready**. By tightening narrative and structure, applying on-page SEO hygiene, and polishing microcopy, you’ll improve engagement and reduce time-to-publish—without committing to a full site overhaul.

## FAQ

**How many pages are included?**  
Up to **5 pages** (≈500–800 words each). Add more in blocks as an add-on.

**Do you handle technical SEO?**  
This package focuses on **on-page SEO** (titles, metas, headings, links). Technical SEO and performance work are out of scope but can be paired with a separate audit.

**Will you implement changes in our CMS?**  
We deliver tracked changes or CMS comments. Hands-on implementation can be added via change order.

**How many revisions are included?**  
**One consolidated round** across all pages. Additional rounds or pages can be scoped.

**What’s the timeline?**  
Typical turnaround is **10 business days** from kickoff, assuming timely feedback.

## Notes

- Best results come from **one consolidated reviewer** and a short voice calibrator  
- Pair with **Messaging Playbook** for deeper repositioning or net-new narrative  
- Accessibility is default: **readability and inclusive language** checks are included
