## Overview

**Explainer Video Starter** distills your product story into a tight, memorable video that teams can use across your site, campaigns, and sales cycles. We handle script, storyboard, animation, VO, music, captions, and export—then package sources so you can iterate later.

### What you’ll get

- A polished **60–90s explainer** that’s on brand and purpose-built for conversion  
- **Cut-downs** for 1:1 and 9:16 to cover socials and shorts  
- **Captions** for accessibility and silent-autoplay contexts  
- A small **usage guide** and **organized source files** for future edits

## Purpose

Package your story into a clear, repeatable asset that educates fast and converts. Success looks like a single, consistent narrative that raises engagement on key pages, improves demo request rates, reduces “what is it?” questions in onboarding, and gives your team a reusable motion foundation for future assets—all accessible via captions and multiple aspect ratios.

## Notes

- Scope includes two consolidated revision rounds across script and visuals  
- Premium VO casting, custom music, live-action filming, and advanced 3D are available as add-ons  
- For faster timelines, lock narrative after Week 1 to reduce back-and-forth  
- We’ll pass through any premium licensing at cost with prior approval

## FAQ

### How long does the process take?

Typical turnaround is **2–3 weeks** from kickoff, assuming timely feedback.

### Can we include product UI?

Yes—provide clean captures or we can create motion composites. A dedicated UI capture add-on is available if you want hands-on support.

### Do you support localization?

We can add subtitle tracks and re-record VO for target languages as an add-on.

### What about licensing?

We use properly licensed music/VO. Premium selections are passed through at cost with approval.
